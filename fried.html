Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice, cooled
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or shrimp (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat.
2. Add beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove from pan and set aside.
3. In the same pan, add minced garlic and chopped green onions. Saut√© until fragrant.
4. Add mixed vegetables and cooked chicken or shrimp (if using). Cook until vegetables are tender.
5. Add cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
6. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, chili flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
7. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and toss everything together until heated through.
8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious fried rice!
