Mutton Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 4 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan, add sliced onions, and fry until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside for garnish.
2. To the remaining onions in the pan, add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Add mutton pieces and cook until they are browned.
4. Add chopped tomatoes, chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, green chilies, mint leaves, coriander leaves, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
5. In a separate pot, layer half of the soaked rice, followed by the cooked mutton mixture. Repeat the layers and top with the reserved fried onions.
6. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is cooked and mutton is tender.
7. Serve hot with raita and enjoy!
