Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until onions are golden brown.
2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
3. Add curry powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well.
4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are well coated with the spices.
5. Pour enough water to cover the chicken pieces. Cover and cook until chicken is tender.
6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
