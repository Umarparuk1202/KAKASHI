Paratto Recipe

Paratto

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add warm water gradually, mixing with your hands until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a thin circle.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Brush it lightly with oil.
  5. Cook each paratto for about 1-2 minutes on each side, or until golden brown spots appear.
  6. Remove from the skillet and serve warm.

Enjoy your delicious Paratto!
