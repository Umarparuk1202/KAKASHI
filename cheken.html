Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons ghee

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of ghee. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  5. Add chicken pieces, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is no longer pink.
  6. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice and cook until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  7. Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the chicken masala. Sprinkle half of the garam masala, coriander leaves, and mint leaves.
  8. Add the remaining rice and sprinkle the rest of the garam masala, coriander leaves, and mint leaves.
  9. Drizzle the remaining ghee over the rice.
  10. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked.
  11. Once done, let it sit for 5 minutes before serving. Serve hot with raita and salad.

Note: Adjust spices according to your taste preferences.
