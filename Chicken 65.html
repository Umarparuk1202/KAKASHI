Chicken 65 Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken pieces
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the mixture and marinate for 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the marinated chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Remove from oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  5. Chicken 65 is ready to serve. Enjoy it hot!
