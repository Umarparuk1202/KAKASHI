Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and sauté until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and sauté for 2 minutes.
3. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are mushy.
4. Add fish fillets and cook for 5-7 minutes until fish is cooked through.
5. Pour coconut milk, simmer for 2-3 minutes, and remove from heat.
6. Serve hot with rice or bread.
